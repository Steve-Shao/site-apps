Important Emissions Testing Information

Standard testing hours for all locations: Monday-Friday 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturday 7:30 AM to 1:00 PM.
Test stations are closed on Sundays and state holidays.

Get your emissions test and license plate sticker in one visit! License plate stickers can be purchased at all High Capacity Drive-Thru Test Stations.

For additional test stations and to find the one nearest you:
• Visit: www.IllinoisAirTeam.net
• Call: 844-258-9071
• Email: info@IllinoisAirTeam.net

Renew Online - Skip The Line!

Take advantage of these convenient renewal options:

  • Online: Visit ILSOS.gov (Use your registration ID and PIN located on your renewal notice)
  • Phone: Call 866-545-9609
  • Mail: Send a check with your license plate number and return the renewal postcard to:
    Renewals Section
    501 S Second St
    Springfield, IL 62756

Go paperless! Sign up to receive your vehicle registration renewal notices by email at ILSOS.gov

About This Resource

I created this site after getting a vehicle registration and emission test notice. When trying to find testing centers, I hit a roadblock. Most search results only showed text listings of locations. Finding the closest center meant searching each one on maps separately. Many sites had wrong info - not official locations or not for Chicago tests. This page fixes that problem by putting all correct locations on a map.
